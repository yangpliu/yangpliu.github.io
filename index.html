About Me

This is the academic homepage of Yang Liu (I publish under Yang P. Liu). In September 2018, I started a PhD at Stanford University in mathematics. Before attending Stanford, I graduated from MIT in May 2018. I am interested in a broad range of topics, including combinatorics, theoretical computer science, and number theory.
