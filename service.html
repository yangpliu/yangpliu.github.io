Math Olympiad

In summer 2017-2018 I was a teaching assistant at the Math Olympiad SUmmer Program. In summer 2019, I was an instructor at the Math Olympiad Summer Program and the Deputy Leader for the USA International Math Olympiad delegation.


SPARC

In summer 2016-2018 I was a junior counselor the Summer Program for Applied Rationality and Cognition. This was an awesome program I attended as a senior in high school, and I encourage everyone to apply!


Harvard-MIT Mathematics Tournament

The Harvard-MIT Mathematics Tournament is a student-run mathematics tournament held alternately at MIT and Harvard, with contests in November and February. I was the problem czar for the November 2015 contest and the February 2017 contest. For the 2017-2018 school year, I was part of the software team. I updated pieces of the registration system and along with Evan Chen designed Hydrogen to assist with remote competitions.
